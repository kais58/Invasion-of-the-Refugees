Invasion 2.0 guide:

During the first 10 minutes of the mission the settings can be changed.
Open the F10 radio menu, and go into the "invasion commands" submenu.
Here you can select different settings for the mission.

small, medium and large group settings dictate how many enemies, both SAMs and aircraft, will be spawned.
"small group" is designed for <10 players
"medium group" is for 10-20 players
"large group" is for >20 players

In addition, the primary objective type can be selected, either a structure (building complex), special (vehicle group) or SAM (SA-10)

Enemy CAP can also be turned off.

Afterwards confirm the settings and start the mission.

If no settings are selected during the first 10 minutes, default settings will be used.
